# AEO SEO Skill Family

This family covers search and answer-engine visibility. It helps Codex audit whether a product, company, or topic can be discovered, understood, cited, and measured across traditional search, AI Overviews, answer engines, and LLM-powered discovery.

## Boundary

AEO SEO owns:

- SEO, AEO, and GEO readiness audits
- AI answer visibility, citations, mentions, share of answer, and query-set tracking
- Topic, question, entity, and source coverage
- Structured data, FAQ, schema, page architecture, and machine-readable evidence
- Zero-click, AI referral, and non-click visibility metric interpretation
- Content briefs and refresh plans for answer-ready pages

AEO SEO does not own:

- ICP, segmentation, positioning, value proposition, channels, launch motion, outbound, PLG, sales, or full GTM strategy
- Product strategy, PRDs, feature prioritization, pricing/package decisions, UX, or prototype scope
- Agent workflow design, MCP/CLI/tool implementation, AGENTS.md contracts, context policy, or agent failure handling

When a request depends on those decisions, AEO SEO should produce the visibility-facing requirements and hand off the source decision to the owning family.

## Skills

| Skill | Status | Purpose |
|---|---:|---|
| `audit-search-visibility` | draft validated | Diagnose SEO, AEO, and GEO readiness, citation gaps, schema/content issues, and measurement baseline. |
| `build-aeo-content-brief` | draft validated | Turn existing positioning, value proposition, and proof into answer-ready content briefs without rewriting GTM messaging. |
| `measure-ai-search-performance` | draft validated | Design query sets, prompt sets, citation tracking, AI referral metrics, and benchmark cadence. |

## AEO SEO Patterns

| Pattern | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `ai-visibility-measurement` | Define citations, mentions, AI referrals, AIO presence, query-set tracking, and share of answer. |
| `answer-ready-content` | Structure existing product facts, positioning, proof, FAQ, schema, and refresh cadence for search and answer engines. |
| `entity-source-credibility` | Check whether an entity has clear facts, external proof, third-party consistency, and verifiable source coverage. |
